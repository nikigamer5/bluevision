Refund Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Digital Products Policy

Due to the digital nature of our products and the immediate access granted upon purchase, all sales are final and non-refundable.

Replacement Guarantee

While we do not offer refunds, we provide a replacement guarantee under the following conditions:

  • Defective Products: If a tool/script is confirmed defective and cannot be fixed
  • Account Issues: If a purchased account is invalid or doesn't work (must be reported within warranty period)
  • Wrong Delivery: If you received the wrong product

Warranty Periods

  • Tools & Scripts: 24-48 hours replacement
  • Discord Tokens: 24 hours - 30 days (varies by product)
  • Game Accounts: 24 hours - 30 days (varies by product)
  • Premium Accounts: 30-180 days (varies by product)

How to Request a Replacement

To request a replacement:

  1. Join our Discord server: discord.gg/38QmF8TKCr
  2. Open a ticket in the #support channel
  3. Provide your order/transaction ID
  4. Describe the issue in detail
  5. Include any screenshots or evidence

Exceptions

Replacements will NOT be provided for:

  • User error or misuse
  • Accounts banned after delivery (unless specified otherwise)
  • Change of mind
  • Reports after warranty period expired

Contact

For any questions about our refund/replacement policy, join our Discord: discord.gg/38QmF8TKCr
